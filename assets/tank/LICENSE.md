`tank_baked.glb` is a derivative of `tank.glb` produced by this project's bake step:
node transforms baked, hull/turret split into two meshes, flat material colors baked
into an embedded palette texture with per-vertex UVs, reoriented barrel-forward (+Z),
rescaled and grounded. Same CC0 license.

`tank_meta.txt` (turret pivot, muzzle point, hull bounds) is generated by the same step.
